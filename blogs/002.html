Lake Tahoe East Shore

The east shore of Lake Tahoe isn't really known for rock climbing compared to the rest of the lake. Most of the climbs feature decomposing granite next to world class views. Anything not involving a large hike has probably already been done, but why move to Tahoe if you aren't down to hike?
